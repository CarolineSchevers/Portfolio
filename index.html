BeCode is a non-profit organisation that offers educational programs for anyone who is motivated for a new start.

The goal of BeCode is to help people find a job in the IT. In a 7-month program BeCode learns you all the basics of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP.
In the last two months you can choose an framework or language to learn on your own.
The coaches will still be available to help you when you're stuck or to give you advice.

Most importantly, BeCode learns you how to search on Google and find the answers yourself. Their motto is: "Google is your best friend".
